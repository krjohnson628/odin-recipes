Fried Tofu

Description

This tofu recipe requires an air fryer. I use an add on for the Instant Pot. The tofu is great for fusion tacos, on salads, or even in quesadillas. It is great for vegans and non-vegans alike.

Ingredients

  • 1 10oz package of firm tofu (Hodo brand preferred)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp neutral high heat oil
  • 2 tsps sesame oil
  • 1.5 tsp garlic powder
  • dash cayenne
  • 1 tsp umami powder (optional, but addds great flavor)
  • pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Cut tofu open and drain.
  2. Cut tofu into 4 rows on the thin way and 6 rows on the wide way, creating 24 total tofu squares.
  3. Put tofus onto a cutting board covered by a paper towel folded in half. Cover the tofus in a paper towel folded in half and then put another cutting board on top of them. Put a weight on top of the cutting board. Press for 30 min - 2 hrs.
  4. Create a marinade by combining the soy sauce, oil, sesame oil, garlic powder, cayenne, and umami powder in a tupperware
  5. Add tofu to the marinade. Marinate for 10 minutes, flipping every few minutes.
  6. Add cornstarch to the marinade. Shake until saucy. If it is chunky, add more soy sauce.
  7. Fry in the air fryer for 10-12 minutes, rotating halfway through.
