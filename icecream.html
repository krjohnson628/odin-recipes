Graham Cracker Ice Cream

Description

This ice cream is loaded with graham crackers and chocolate chunks. It is made using an ice cream maker.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 1 1/2 cups half & half
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 8 graham crackers
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 dark chocolate bar
