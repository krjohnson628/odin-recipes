Graham Cracker Ice Cream

Description

This ice cream is loaded with graham crackers and chocolate chunks. It is made using an ice cream maker.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 1 1/2 cups half & half
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 8 graham crackers
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 dark chocolate bar

Steps

  1. Crush graham crackers in their sleeve. I prefer some chunks and some finely ground pieces.
  2. Combine heavy whipping cream and brown sugar in a medium bowl until sugar crystals have dissolved.
  3. Add half & half, crushed graham cracker pieces, salt, and vanilla to the heavy whipping cream and sugar mixture. Stir until combined.
  4. Pour the mixture into the ice cream maker and churn about 20 minutes (follow the instructions on your machine).
  5. While ice cream is churning, break or cut the chocolate bar into the desired chunk size.
  6. When the ice cream has almost finished churning, add the chocolate chunks.
  7. Transfer ice cream to a container and cover. Freeze for about 4 hours before serving.
